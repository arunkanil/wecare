Our Departments
to choose from
General medicine
The General medicine department at Wecare is inclusive with general practitioners who are highly qualified with over 19 years of experience in preventive and family medicine and management aspects of common medical conditions in individuals of all ages. The department is an integral and essential part of Wecare emphasizing disease prevention and health promotion. Our team of best general physicians in UAE offers a comprehensive clinical program and treatment to various health conditions by ensuring quality care and comfort to patients.
General surgery
Our specialized general surgeons with tremendously vast experiences provide its patients with the services employing cutting-edge surgical care. The general surgery team has its expertise in conservative and operative management in various operative surgical problems with astoundingly high rates of skill and professionalism and providing complete patient safety and satisfaction.
Internal medicine
We have specialized internal medicine doctors with over 10 years of experience in dealing with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of acute and chronic health conditions, lifestyle diseases, and internal diseases. The team works in collaboration with other specialties for a thorough evaluation of the patient’s condition and provides personalized and effective care and ensures a complete recovery
Obstetrics/Gynecology
Wecare provides one-stop healthcare for women of all ages. The obstetrics/gynecology team has the perfect blend of skill, expertise, and experience. We offer comprehensive health care across the female lifespan starting from the pre-pregnancy period to menopause and beyond, provided with advanced technologies in diagnosing and treating gynecologic conditions. Our special team of academically oriented and well-trained young gynecologists are consistently setting high standards in the specialty by reaching out to patients with perpetual and uninterrupted care.
Pediatrics
Care of children in health and illness is an integral part of any health care service. Our department of caring and compassionate pediatrics led by a pediatric specialist offers a wide range of clinical services and interacts closely with patients ensures that they are nurturing them with their full potential. The specialized center in UAE provides personalized and inclusive care for the entire range of pediatric specialties and helps children to lead happy, healthy lives while providing comfort and convenience for their families.
Dental
The department of Dentistry at Wecare is equipped with the latest technologies to offer a wide range of dental care from basic diagnosis, routine dental procedures to advanced dental services with a friendly atmosphere. Our expertise and exceptional service have put us on the map as one of the most sought after places for dental care in UAE for providing dental treatments at affordable prices. Our team of skillful and experienced dentists offers expert care and services to patients of all age groups and also with special needs by treating all kinds of oral and dental disorders and sculpting a better smile to lead a better life.
Orthodontics
Orthodontics, also known as dentofacial orthopedics, is a special field of dentistry dealing with irregularities of the teeth and their correction. The department of orthodontics at Wecare is known for creating beautiful, healthy smiles all over the UAE. Our highly professional team of specialists provides the best possible orthodontic care to our patients using the latest in orthodontic technology with better and faster results. Our team recognizes that every patient is different and so are their needs, this makes us go out of our way to make them smile from inside out.
Orthopedics
Orthopedic health is critical for a quality life. Wecare offers adults and children the full spectrum of orthopedic services like modern diagnostics, treatments for injury of varying severity, specialized services for comprehensive management. Our team strives to ensure that the highest level of quality care, quick and personalized attention is given for the patients to positively impact their health, well-being, and healing.
Homeopathy
Wecare offers a well-equipped homeopathy treatment facility that provides scientific, research-based, and professional services to people across the UAE. Homeopathy, being an alternative system of medicine has gained substantial popularity mainly due to its clinical efficiency. Scientific basis and no side effects. Our team of practitioners who are experienced in ayurvedic/homeopathy medication and management is committed to effective, efficient, and ethical offering of homeopathic treatments for a range of chronic and recurring diseases.
Clinical Psychology
The clinical psychology department at Wecare is equipped with highly qualified and well-trained Psychiatrists and psychologists providing psychotherapeutic interventions to counsel people suffering from mental illnesses or emotional distress. The team expertise is particularly channeled into understanding, diagnosing, and subsequently help people through various ages treating any behavioral issues, mental distress, or disorder in children, teens, and adults.
Laboratory Sciences
A well-equipped laboratory is the backbone of the healthcare industry. Wecare offers a laboratory on par with international standards with the support of the latest advancements in technology, new methodologies, and high-end equipment that render a better and error-free diagnosis in the shortest time possible. With a team of highly experienced doctors, technicians, and ancillary staff, the department closely works with all other departments to cater services to the required patients on time.
